About Our Chapter

Who We Are

A community of students who love to create, explore, and push what’s possible in computing. We bring together makers, innovators, and learners united by curiosity and a drive to grow.

Our Mission

To make technical growth accessible, inspiring, and community-driven. We believe students learn best when they have the space, support, and tools to experiment and build together.

We are called an ACM Chapter because we’re a local home for the Association for Computing Machinery, which is the world's biggest community for tech pros and students. Being an official 'chapter' means we’re plugged into a huge global network of researchers and developers. It’s our way of bringing the best of the computer science world right here to our campus for anyone who loves coding and technology.

You can become a member by joining the ACM Chapter on Titan Atlas! It's free... no fee!

The only requirement is curiousity about Computer Science, Informatics, Physics, Math, and related topics ;)
